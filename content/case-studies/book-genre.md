# Multimodal Book Genre Classification & Clustering

## Goal
Use book cover images + titles to represent and cluster books into genres/themes.

## Methods
- Image embeddings from CNN backbone
- Title embeddings from NLP model
- Fusion and clustering (KMeans/HDBSCAN), with qualitative inspection
