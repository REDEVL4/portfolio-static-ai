# Disease Networks for Disease Clustering (GCN, Unsupervised)

## Goal
Model disease–gene relationships as a graph and learn embeddings for unsupervised disease clustering.

## Approach
- Construct disease–gene graph features
- Train GCN embeddings
- Cluster in embedding space; inspect and validate clusters

## Next improvements
- Benchmark against external biomedical ontologies/datasets
- Add stronger quantitative evaluation and ablations
