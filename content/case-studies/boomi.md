# Boomi Integration Reliability and Automation Work

## Context
This work reflects the kind of integration and runtime engineering I handled in enterprise environments where reliability, supportability, and observability mattered as much as feature delivery.

## What I Built
- High-availability Dell Boomi integrations across Salesforce, NetSuite, on-prem systems, and fulfillment partners
- Event-driven APIs, listeners, and asynchronous workflows for real-time synchronization
- Schema validation and control checks across multiple ingestion pipelines
- Runtime health checks, restart automations, and support runbooks to reduce recurring failures

## Scale and Impact
- Supported 10M plus daily transactions across distributed enterprise runtimes
- Sustained 99.9 percent SLA uptime for business-critical workflows
- Reduced incident triage time by 40 percent through Splunk and AppDynamics observability across 12 distributed services
- Reduced onboarding time for new engineers by 50 percent with better automation and support documentation

## Why It Matters
This work is a strong example of reliability engineering in integration-heavy systems: less glamorous than greenfield product work, but essential to stable operations and scalable partner data exchange.
